# HW3 Project Analysis & Deliverable Roadmap

## 1) Core Deliverables (Checklist)

- **HTML Sandbox Page**
  - Create a standalone web page where you can test HTML code.
  - Link it from your HW3 answer page.

- **Fishbone (Ishikawa) Diagram**
  - Create a highly detailed analysis of the problem:
    - "I want to get as much as possible out of taking the course [Course Number]."
  - Include sub-sub-branches.
  - Format can be hand-drawn (photo) or made with a digital tool.

- **Written Analysis**
  - Provide a text-based evaluation of the causes in your diagram.
  - Identify which causes are reasonable and which can be excluded.
  - Suggest potential improvements.

- **AI Socratic Transcript**
  - Include the full transcript of your interaction with an AI using Socratic questioning about fishbone diagrams.

- **Reflective Commentary**
  - Evaluate HW2 (what worked and what didn’t).
  - Critique the Socratic AI experience and describe your future prompting strategy.

- **Homepage Status**
  - Add a link on your homepage to HW3.
  - Clearly indicate "Ready to be Graded."

## 2) Expectations & Standards (Rules)

- **Anonymity**
  - Do not include your name or student ID number anywhere on the assignment.

- **Security**
  - Do not ask for sensitive information in forms (passwords, credit cards, etc.) to avoid phishing warnings.

- **Tooling Documentation**
  - If you use a digital tool for the diagram, state:
    - tool name,
    - source,
    - your rating.

- **Grading Visibility**
  - Put written analysis as text on the page (not only in an image), so graders don’t miss it.

## 3) Administrative Notes

- **Late Work**
  - If submitted late, send an email so the instructor checks your site.
  - Their internal spreadsheet and Blackboard may update at different times.

- **Future Utility**
  - Design the HTML sandbox as a standalone page for future use beyond this homework.
