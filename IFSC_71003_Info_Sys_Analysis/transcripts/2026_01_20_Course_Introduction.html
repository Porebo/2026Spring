6. The 50–100 Grading Scale

The Problem with 0–100

Daniel demonstrated why the traditional 0–100 scale is mathematically unfair using two hypothetical students:

StudentCourse 1Course 2ResultGPA
M100s on everything0s on everythingA + F2.0 (C average)
N100s first half, 0s second100s first half, 0s second50% = F + F0.0
Both students learned the same amount of material, but Student N gets a 0.0 GPA while Student M gets a 2.0 GPA. This is fundamentally unfair.

The 50–100 Fix

StudentCourse 1Course 2ResultGPA
M100s50s (minimum)A + F2.0
NAvg of 100 & 50 = 75%Avg of 100 & 50 = 75%C + C2.0
"With the 50–100 scale, both students now get the same GPA — which is fair, because they both learned the same amount of material."

Reference: "The Case Against the Zero" article (linked in syllabus).

8. AI Usage & Academic Integrity

AI Policy

AI use is allowed but not unlimited. The key distinction:

Over-reliance (no credit): Copy question into AI → paste answer into homework → submit.
Proper use: Learn what the AI is doing, not just use what it does.
  • Daniel uses AI himself for creating homework games and coding assistance
  • Thomas Wallace raised the difficulty of detecting AI-generated HTML/CSS
  • Daniel's approach: "I want you to learn, I want you to use AI, and I want the course to be a good learning experience"
  • Tip: Ask AI to explain things "for dummies" for clear, understandable answers

Collaboration Policy

"The right way to treat your friend is to give them hints... they'll learn something." — Daniel Berleant
  • Encouraged: Discuss assignments and problems with classmates, give hints
  • Prohibited: Copying answers, sharing completed work
  • Academic dishonesty goes through official university process

9. InfinityFree Setup

Students will host their assignments on a free web server:

  1. Go to InfinityFree.com → sign up → create website
  2. Your URL will be: yourname.infinityfreeapp.com
  3. Alternative hosting platforms are allowed (must support PHP and JavaScript)
Important tips:
  • Use lowercase letters only for all file names (case sensitivity)
  • Avoid spaces in file names
  • Save frequently — the file manager can time out
  • If it times out: Ctrl+A → Ctrl+C → re-login → paste
  • Don't confuse InfinityFree.com (the company) with InfinityFreeApp.com (where pages live)
  • Give your site a name that can't be guessed by other students
  • Account creation may take up to 72 hours (but usually much faster)
