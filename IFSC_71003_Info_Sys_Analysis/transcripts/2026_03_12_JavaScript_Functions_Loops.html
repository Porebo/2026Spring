4. Loop Techniques

48:00–65:00

While vs. For

  • While loops emphasize condition-first thinking; for loops bundle initialization, test, and increment in one header.
  • Students converted a simple while into a for by pulling the counter setup and increment into the new clause.

Syntax Hygiene

  • Keep braces aligned, semicolons intentional, and clause commas out of loop headers.
