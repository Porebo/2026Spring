Battery Factory Metrics

  • Before the layout change, the battery plant’s operator traveled 113 ft taking roughly 440 minutes per cycle; moving stations cut the trace to 33 ft and 130 minutes, showcasing how diagrams quantify distance + time payoffs.

Insight vs. prescription: Spaghetti diagrams expose pain points but never guarantee the fix—pair every proposed shortcut with a worker health check so “fewer feet” does not quietly increase strain.
