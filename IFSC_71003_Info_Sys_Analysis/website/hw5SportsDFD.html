Q5: Sport-Themed Website Design + Data Flow Diagram

← Back to Homework 5
Website Concept

Proposed site: CourtPulse, a basketball fan portal where users can track teams, compare players, and receive game-day alerts.

  • Users can create profiles and choose favorite teams.
  • The system aggregates stats, schedules, and standings from a sports data provider.
  • Fans can save watchlists and get notifications before tipoff.
  • Admins moderate posts and maintain featured stories.
