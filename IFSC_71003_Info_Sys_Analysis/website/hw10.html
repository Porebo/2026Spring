Part 2: Evaluation Dimensions Applied
  • System: advising and registration support workflow.
  • Criteria: response time, satisfaction, rework rate.
  • Process: baseline, pilot, post-change comparison.
  • Evaluators: mixed internal team plus independent reviewer.
  • Timing: pre-peak, peak, and post-peak measurement windows.
