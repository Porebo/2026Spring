## Grading & Policies

### Scores
- Scored on **50%–100% scale** (not 0–100%)
- 100-pt HW scored 50–100; 20-pt attendance scored 10–20
- Reference: "The Case Against the Zero" by Douglas B. Reeves

### Lateness
- **−10 points** if up to 1 week late
- **−20 points** (two reductions) if very late
- No credit for multiple assignments rushed at end of semester

### Attendance
- 20 pts each time attendance is taken
- eLearning campus: no attendance requirement
- Students not attending, not turning in work, and not communicating will be **administratively withdrawn**

### Academic Integrity & AI
- **Over-reliance on AI** → no credit for that part
- Work must be your own; copying = no credit even if modified
- Encouraged: discussion, hints, useful information between students
- Prohibited: sharing actual answers
