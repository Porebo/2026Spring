## Rapid Prototyping Topic
- Daniel described rapid prototypes as front-end look-and-feel versions of a future system.
- A rapid prototype can resemble the final product without containing databases, advanced back-end logic, or full production infrastructure.
- Students were asked to connect the rapid prototyping discussion to their project and submit a screenshot-based written response.

## Project Emphasis
