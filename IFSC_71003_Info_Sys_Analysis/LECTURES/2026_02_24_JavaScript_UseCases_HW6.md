## AI Usage Tips (from Daniel)
- AIs tend to give **overly long, advanced answers** by default
- Say **"be very brief"** or **"answer for dummies"** to get simpler responses
- If still too long: **"give me another answer, much briefer than your previous one"**
- AIs are **strong with JavaScript** (trained on lots of web data, low hallucination rate)
- Tell the AI how to teach you: **"teach me to teach you"**
- Ask **pointed, specific questions** about individual tokens/keywords in code
