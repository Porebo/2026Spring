Technical Highlights

  • Requests + Status Codes: Shows why some sites (GeeksForGeeks, UALR) block generic traffic and how to pivot to friendlier domains like Census.gov.
  • Beautiful Soup: Pretty-prints HTML, locates div/class containers, and enables selective extraction rather than dumping the entire DOM.
  • Selenium: Necessary for JS-heavy pages but tricky inside Google Colab without custom drivers; easier via local .py execution.
  • lxml / urllib: Offer XPath querying and URL handling; success rates vary by target, reinforcing the need for experimentation.
  • Automation: Demonstrated scheduling libraries and GUI automation to run scrapers overnight or on recurring intervals.
