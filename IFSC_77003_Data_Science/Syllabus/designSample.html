2. Single-Column (Blog Style)

The Art of the Long Form

This design focuses entirely on readability. By restricting the line length (usually between 60-80 characters per line), it prevents eye fatigue. It is the gold standard for long articles and storytelling where interruptions (like boxes) are not wanted.

It creates a linear flow that encourages the reader to stay focused on the narrative from start to finish.
